How to Play

The battlefield is designed based on world war II

Different country united as a Axis or Allies, You can choose which side you are fighting for!

Event&Quest

There are many Event may happen during the war game! For example:

Western Front (Europe)

Event : Dunkirk evacuation

Condition : If the France loss the Calais the Dunkirk evacuation will happen.

Eastern Front (Europe)

Event : Battle of Stalingrad

Condition : The Soviet Union successfully counterattack from the moscow. Battle of Stalingrad will happan.

Asia - Pacific War

Event : Battle of Iwo Jima

Condition : The USA successfully land on the Japan.Battle of Iwo Jima event will happan.
